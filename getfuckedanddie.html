The premise behind this piece was the idea of a party on the eve of the apocalypse. The poster is made up of jokes that expand on the irony of this idea: a $40 entry fee, chalk outlines of ravers, a cheesy fire decal with an actual forest fire in it, etc. The bulk of the composition is taken up by this large, upturned figure, which is a detail from the altar of the Sistine Chapel. However, in this version the man isn't being pulled down to hell by the demons, he is being body slammed.

12" × 18", three-color poster. Pantone 813U (neon pink), Pantone 803U (neon yellow), and black. Color separations done for each individual image in photoshop.
