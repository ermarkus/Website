The Apocalypse Soirée, a party hosted the night of the end of the world. The poster consists of a detail of Michelangelo's Sistine Chapel, a figure being dragged down into hell. However, the image is flipped, so that the demon is not dragging him down to hell, but RKOing him to hell. There are a lot of other jokes like this in the poster. Three exclamation points in the top right corner, crime-scene chalk outline of people dancing, a cheesy fire vector with an actual forest fire in it, the $40 entry fee, etc.

24" × 36", three-color poster. Pantone 813U (neon pink), Pantone 803U (neon yellow), and black. Color separations done for each individual image in photoshop.
