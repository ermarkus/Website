ABOUT

The premise behind this piece was the idea of a party on the eve of the apocalypse. The bulk of the composition is taken up by this large, upturned figure, which is a detail from the altar of the Sistine Chapel. However, in this version the man isn't being pulled down to hell, he's being body slammed to hell.
