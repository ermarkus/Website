Freelance Work

Blair Academy, a long-standing institution located in northwestern New Jersey, commissioned me to design a reading exprience for its Fall 2022 Fundraising Brochure.

Soon after Blair's marketing team sent me the project assets, I suggested that we format the brochure into an accordion fold. The reason being, an accordion fold allows the reader to browse the donation options page-by-page and browse them all at once laid-flat. The marketing team greenlit the idea, and I went to work on the page layout.

I used a clean, two-color pallete, set the text into a clear heirarchy, and kept unecessary graphic elements to a minimum. My strategy was to communicate the sophistication of Blair Academy's brand identity through simplicity. The project was finished within three weeks.
