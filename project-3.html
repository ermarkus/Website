Client Work

Since graduating in 2020, I have been working freelance for Blair Academy, a small private school in northern New Jersey. I design print collateral such as fundraisers (shown here), invitations, and brochures, as well as some digital assets. I use this work as an opportunity to keep my design skills sharp, and to improve my skills for layout design.

I was brought in to conceptualize and execute a form that best fit this fundraising copy. I decided that an accordion fold expressed the message of the copy while also giving the audience an engaging reading experience. I communicated with Blair throughout the design process to ensure their satisfaction.
