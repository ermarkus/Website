College Bubble

16in x 16in

Given a prompt to create a system of logographs for a chosen subject, I chose to represent the daily grind of the college student.

I began noting how I spent my time throughout the day and, after collecting my data, began to illustrate the logographs. I chose to represent activities that college students oftentimes do alone: eat, shower, clean, walk in the rain, walk in the snow, think, sleep, and take notes.

Logographs ( college students )

After finalizing the designs, I placed the logographs around the face of a prepurchased wall clock and jammed the reset button so that its hands spun around endlessly instead of telling the time of day. This choice emphasized the repetitious feeling of college life and transformed the piece, as a whole, into a design object.
