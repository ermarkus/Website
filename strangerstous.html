ABOUT

Strangers to Us was a student show at Boston University’s College of Fine Arts. I contributed an artwork and designed all of the exhibition’s graphics. The wall text for my piece, Shirtless Men Talking to Attractive Woman is as follows:

Michael, Tyler, Nick, and Matt each agreed to have a one-on-one conversation with an attractive woman on camera, and were not given any information about the situation nor the woman prior to their conversation. Later, as they each walked to the filming location, they received two pieces of information and one instruction: This woman is single (she was not), this woman is looking for a partner (she was not), and they had to take off their shirt.

Wall vinyl, labels, various collateral, and inkjet prints.
