Student exhibition at Boston University’s College of Fine Arts. I contributed an artwork, Shirtless Men Talking to Attractive Woman, as well as all exhibition graphics. For the Shirtless piece, I asked several people if they’d be willing to have a conversation with an attractive stranger. I told them that this woman was single and looking for a partner. Neither was true. Those who agreed came to the filming location, and immediately before they walked into the room, I surprised them and made them take off their shirts.

Wall vinyl, labels, various collateral, and inkjet prints.
