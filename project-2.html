Boston Museums

Size: 3x4"

I was given a prompt to design a travel guide for Boston tourists. I chose to center my project around the various museums in Boston because visiting a museum is a perfect day-trip for a tourist.

I wanted to convey the theme of subway travel in my cover design. I did this by casting the individual characters of the title as different destinations on the page, using lines to carry the reader's eye from one letter to the next, like a subway line carries you from one place to another.

Considering the audience, I included the address and hours of operation for each museum, as well as a short description of the atmosphere. The final product could be unfolded to show a subway map of the Boston area on the back.
